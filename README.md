## 🧠 About Me

I'm an **AI & XR Engineer** with a passion for building intelligent systems that bridge the physical and virtual worlds. My work spans:

- **AI/ML**: LLMs, VLMs, RAG pipelines, agentic workflows, computer vision (YOLOv8, CLIP), deep learning.
- **XR/VR/MR/AR**: Unity (XR Toolkit, AR Foundation), immersive simulations, WebXR, spatial computing.
- **Robotics & Simulation**: ROS2 (Humble), Gazebo, Nav2, sensor fusion, digital twins, industrial automation.
- **IoT & Embedded**: ESP32, ESP8266, edge AI (TensorFlow Lite), real‑time telemetry, drone communication.

Currently, I'm prototyping a **Vision-Language-Action (VLA) robotics system** and building **VR‑based universities** that transform engineering education.
