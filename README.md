## 🧠 About Me

I'm an AI & Embedded Systems engineer focused on building intelligent systems that operate at the intersection of **perception, autonomy, and real-world impact**. My work spans:

- **Embedded AI**: TensorFlow Lite on edge devices, real-time object detection, sensor fusion
- **Robotics & Simulation**: ROS2, Gazebo, Nav2, digital twins, industrial automation
- **XR/VR/MR**: Unity, immersive learning environments, spatial computing
- **IoT & Drones**: ESP32, telemetry, autonomous navigation, drone systems

> **Current focus**: Building production-ready vision-language-action (VLA) systems for autonomous navigation and road safety.
